Help & Controls

Explore the origami dungeon in first-person. Avoid monsters unless prepared!

  • Move: W/A/S/D or Arrow Keys
  • Pause/Resume: Space
  • Inventory: 🎒 button or type "inventory" in the prompt
  • Click the 2D map to path to a tile; enable confirmation in Settings
  • Stay out of sight; monsters notice you if they have line of sight
  • Mouse wheel over map: zoom
  • Theme toggle: sun/moon button

Tip: Corners and doorways can block light and line-of-sight—sneak smart.
